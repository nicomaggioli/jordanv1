Jordan's 50th

Come celebrate Jordan turning 50 in his favorite place, Ogunquit, Maine. Over the years, it has become his happy place—a spot to unwind, recharge, and enjoy simple things like beach days, ocean air, and great company. It felt like the perfect place to mark this milestone.

If you'd like to make a full weekend of it, we'd love to have you. If you can only join for the Saturday celebration, that works too.

We'll kick things off Friday afternoon with happy hour drinks at Barnacle Billy's in Perkins Cove around 5:30, followed by dinner in town that evening. Saturday will start with a relaxing day at the beach, followed by a birthday bash with great food, music, and plenty of fun.

It's going to be a laid-back, adults-only celebration filled with good vibes, laughter, and unforgettable memories.
